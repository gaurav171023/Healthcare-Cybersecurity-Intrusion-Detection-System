Detected Attack Types

DoS/DDoS Attacks

Denial of Service attacks that overwhelm network resources and disrupt services.

Examples: Neptune, Smurf, Teardrop, Pod, Back
Probe Attacks

Reconnaissance attacks that scan for vulnerabilities and gather system information.

Examples: Satan, Portsweep, Nmap, Ipsweep
R2L (Remote-to-Local)

Unauthorized access from remote machines to gain local access to victim systems.

Examples: Warezclient, Warezmaster, FTP_write, Guess_passwd
U2R (User-to-Root)

Privilege escalation attacks where attackers gain root access from user accounts.

Examples: Buffer_overflow, Loadmodule, Perl, Rootkit
